Dashboard Glossary

Sections

Allocator DatabaseAll Form 5500 filers in one table. Firms with a 9AT signal show their connection upfront. Filter by signal type, enrichment priority, or schedule data availability.
9AT ConnectionThe column shown for every row — firms with no 9AT link show a dash, firms with a match show signal chips and the specific connection.
Mark ContactedCheck the box on a card or in the drawer to mark a firm as already reached out to. Dims the card and lets you filter by outreach status. Stored locally in this browser.
Export CSV / Copy EmailsBoth act on the current filtered view (not just the visible page) — export every matching firm to a spreadsheet, or copy every email found for that view to paste into a mail client.

Allocator Profile Drawer

IdentityName, EIN, plan, location, participant count, and enrichment status.
9AT Relationship MapEvery signal that fired for this firm — what schedule it came from and what was matched.
Service ProvidersAll firms this plan paid fees to (Schedule C Part 1, all 3 items), with 9AT adviser/fund matches where found.
Fund HoldingsDFE pooled fund investments from Schedule D Part 2, matched to 9AT funds where possible.

Signal Types

Sched CTier 1. Plan paid fees to this adviser (Schedule C Part 1).
Sched DTier 1. Plan invests in a DFE pooled fund linked to a 9AT adviser or fund (Schedule D Part 2).
Sched HTier 1. Plan's large-plan Schedule H discloses this as an investment manager.
DirectTier 2 — speculative. Firm name closely matches a 9AT adviser name. Verify before outreach.

Enrichment Priority

✓ READY (EMAIL)Tier 1 signal + real email found — either on file already or found by AI enrichment. Outreach now.
★ ENRICHTier 1 signal but no email yet, and AI enrichment hasn't been run. Use AI enrichment or Hunter.io — high ROI.
⚠ ENRICHED · NO EMAILTier 1 signal — AI enrichment already ran for this firm but didn't find a usable email. Try Hunter.io or manual research, or retry AI enrichment.
· LOWTier 2 signal + email. Verify before outreach.
— SKIPTier 2 signal, no contact. Low ROI.
